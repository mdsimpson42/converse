If you are concerned about the wellbeing of a colleague, or your team in general, then there are a few things we can recommend.

Consider becoming a Mental Health First Aider

By training as a Mental Health First Aider (MHFA), you are equipping yourself to help support colleagues in a crisis, and to advocate for better mental health in your workplace. We particularly recommend this if you plan to be a team lead or line manager in the future.

You can find more information on the MHFA association websites:

  • MHFA England
  • Scottish MHFA
  • MHFA Wales
  • AMH in Northern Ireland

Courses are also offered by a number of charities and organisations, and may even be available through your institution.
